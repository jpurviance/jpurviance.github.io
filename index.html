John Purviance

I'm a Sr. Software Engineer and Python Guild Leader @ Bloomberg in NYC. I am currently building Bloomberg's next-generation object storage solution (Bloomberg's internal alternative to AWS S3). I believe that "Python is the second best tool for any problem." I've been fortunate to reach hundreds of our engineers through my workshops as a Guild Leader. Currently, I'm building our Caching solution for Bloomberg's Python TCP Services. When I'm not working, I am outside capturing moments with photography.
